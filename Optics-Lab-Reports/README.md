### 1. Verification of Reflection & Refraction Laws (PW1)
* **Focus:** Experimental verification of Snell's and Descartes laws using a plane diopter.
* **Key Findings:** Confirmed the linear relationship between $\sin(i)$ and $\sin(r)$, determining the refractive index of PMMA as $n=1.39 \pm 0.06$.
* **Submission Date:** November 11, 2025.

### 2. Spectroscopic Study of the Prism (PW2)
* **Focus:** Investigating light refraction through a prism to determine the refractive index via the minimum deviation method.
* **Key Findings:** Successfully calculated the refractive index as $n \approx 1.50$, consistent with typical glass properties.
* **Submission Date:** December 15, 2025.

### 3. Characterization of Thin Lenses (PW3)
* **Focus:** Determining focal length using Bessel's two-position method and the object-at-infinity method.
* **Key Findings:** Results showed high accuracy with relative errors generally below 4%.
* **Submission Date:** December 16, 2025.

### 4. Wave Optics: Fresnel Mirrors (PW4)
* **Focus:** Investigating interference phenomena and determining the He-Ne laser wavelength.
* **Key Findings:** Calculated the wavelength to be approximately $\lambda \approx 6.3 \times 10^{-7} \text{ m}$, in good agreement with theoretical values.
* **Submission Date:** December 20, 2025.
